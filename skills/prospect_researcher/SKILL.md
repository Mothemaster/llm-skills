# Prospect Researcher

## Purpose
Use this skill to gather relevant facts about prospects for smarter outreach and account strategy.

## Inputs
- Prospect name or company
- Research purpose
- Optional industry, geography, or role

## Process
1. Identify the prospect accurately.
2. Gather business context.
3. Find relevant pain points, triggers, or initiatives.
4. Turn research into outreach-ready notes.

## Output
- Prospect brief
- Personalization points
- Risks or unknowns
- Outreach angles

## Quality bar
- Focus on useful relevance.
- Avoid filler facts.
- Keep it actionable for sales or partnerships.
