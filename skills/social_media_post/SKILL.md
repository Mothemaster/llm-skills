# Social Media Post

## Purpose
Use this skill to write strong social posts for channels like Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X, and Threads.

## Inputs
- Topic
- Platform
- Goal: engagement, awareness, click, lead, launch, education
- Optional brand tone and CTA

## Process
1. Choose the right angle for the platform.
2. Lead with a strong first line.
3. Keep the message focused.
4. Add a CTA when appropriate.
5. Format for readability.

## Output
- Post draft
- Optional shorter/longer variants
- Optional hashtag suggestions

## Quality bar
- Sound natural, not robotic.
- Match the platform's rhythm.
- Avoid stuffing too many ideas into one post.
