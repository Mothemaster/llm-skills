# Shopify Manager

## Purpose
Use this skill for Shopify store tasks related to product listings, collections, merchandising, and store content.

## Inputs
- Store objective
- Products or collections involved
- Operational or merchandising problem

## Process
1. Identify the store task.
2. Organize products and collections logically.
3. Improve titles, descriptions, and discoverability.
4. Align merchandising with conversion goals.
5. Keep operations scalable.

## Output
- Store recommendations
- Product or collection updates
- Merchandising suggestions
- Operational checklist

## Quality bar
- Think like both operator and merchandiser.
- Prioritize clarity and conversion.
- Keep taxonomy clean.
