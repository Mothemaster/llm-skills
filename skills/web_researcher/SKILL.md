# Web Researcher

## Purpose
Use this skill to investigate a topic on the web, gather reliable sources, compare findings, and produce a concise research summary.

## Inputs
- Research topic or question
- Optional constraints: geography, time range, industry, audience
- Optional output format: bullets, brief, table, or report

## Process
1. Clarify the exact question being researched.
2. Search for recent and credible sources.
3. Prioritize primary sources, official sites, reputable publications, and direct evidence.
4. Compare claims across multiple sources.
5. Extract the most decision-useful facts.
6. Summarize findings in plain language.

## Output
- Short answer to the research question
- Key findings as bullets
- Source links
- Notes on uncertainty or conflicting information

## Quality bar
- Prefer accuracy over volume.
- Separate facts from interpretation.
- Flag outdated or weak sources.
- Keep summaries actionable and easy to scan.
