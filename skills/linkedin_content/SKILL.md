# LinkedIn Content

## Purpose
Use this skill to write LinkedIn content that feels credible, clear, and relevant to professional readers.

## Inputs
- Topic
- Goal
- Audience
- Optional personal story, brand voice, or CTA

## Process
1. Find the professional angle.
2. Open with a strong first line.
3. Structure the post for readability.
4. Keep the insight concrete.
5. Close with a clear takeaway or CTA.

## Output
- LinkedIn post draft
- Optional hook variants
- Optional carousel or thread adaptation

## Quality bar
- Avoid fake thought leadership clichés.
- Be specific and useful.
- Write like a real person with expertise.
