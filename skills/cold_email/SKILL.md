# Cold Email

## Purpose
Use this skill to write outbound emails that feel relevant, credible, and easy to respond to.

## Inputs
- Offer
- Target prospect
- Personalization points
- CTA
- Optional tone or length preference

## Process
1. Lead with relevance.
2. Keep the email short.
3. Make one clear point.
4. Ask for one simple next step.

## Output
- Subject lines
- Email draft
- Optional follow-up sequence

## Quality bar
- Personalization must be specific.
- Avoid hype and empty praise.
- Make replying easy.
