# Marketing Research

## Purpose
Use this skill to understand a market, identify competitors, and surface insights that improve positioning and messaging.

## Inputs
- Product, category, or market
- Region or customer segment
- Research goal: competitor review, ICP insight, positioning, trends, pricing, etc.

## Process
1. Define the market and target segment.
2. Identify direct and indirect competitors.
3. Review messaging, offers, pricing, and distribution.
4. Look for customer pain points and unmet needs.
5. Distill strategic opportunities and risks.

## Output
- Market snapshot
- Competitor comparison
- Audience insights
- Messaging or positioning recommendations
- Risks and opportunities

## Quality bar
- Be specific, not abstract.
- Separate observation from recommendation.
- Highlight what matters for decision-making.
