# FAQ Generator

## Purpose
Use this skill to build FAQ content that preempts common questions and reduces confusion.

## Inputs
- Product, service, process, or policy details
- Target audience
- Optional categories

## Process
1. Identify the highest-likelihood questions.
2. Group by theme.
3. Answer directly and concisely.
4. Remove overlap.

## Output
- FAQ list
- Categorized questions and answers
- Optional webpage-ready format

## Quality bar
- Questions should sound real.
- Answers should be direct and useful.
- Avoid vague marketing language.
