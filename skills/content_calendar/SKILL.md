# Content Calendar

## Purpose
Use this skill to plan content across days or weeks with clear themes, formats, and publishing cadence.

## Inputs
- Time period
- Channels
- Goals
- Campaigns or launches
- Content pillars

## Process
1. Define content goals.
2. Group topics into pillars.
3. Balance formats and channels.
4. Build a realistic posting schedule.
5. Tie each item to an outcome.

## Output
- Calendar table with date, topic, format, platform, CTA, owner, or status
- Optional campaign notes

## Quality bar
- Be practical and sustainable.
- Keep variety without losing focus.
- Align content with business goals.
