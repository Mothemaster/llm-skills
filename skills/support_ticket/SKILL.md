# Support Ticket

## Purpose
Use this skill to draft support responses that solve the issue or move it forward efficiently.

## Inputs
- Customer message
- Product or service context
- Relevant policy or troubleshooting steps

## Process
1. Identify the actual issue.
2. Acknowledge the customer's experience.
3. Give the fix, next step, or clarification.
4. Keep the response easy to follow.

## Output
- Support reply draft
- Optional macro version
- Optional escalation note

## Quality bar
- Be empathetic without sounding scripted.
- Avoid jargon unless necessary.
- Focus on resolution.
