# Wiki Generator

## Purpose
Use this skill to turn notes, transcripts, docs, or messy content into clean internal documentation.

## Inputs
- Source material
- Intended audience
- Desired format: SOP, overview, FAQ, troubleshooting, glossary, onboarding page

## Process
1. Identify the document goal.
2. Organize content into a logical structure.
3. Remove redundancy and ambiguity.
4. Add headings, steps, notes, and examples where useful.
5. Keep terminology consistent.

## Output
- Clean wiki page in markdown or doc style
- Optional sections: purpose, scope, steps, examples, FAQs, troubleshooting

## Quality bar
- Optimize for clarity and reuse.
- Use scannable structure.
- Avoid filler language.
