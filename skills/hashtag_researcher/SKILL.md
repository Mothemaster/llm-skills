# Hashtag Researcher

## Purpose
Use this skill to find relevant hashtag sets for discoverability and topical alignment.

## Inputs
- Topic
- Platform
- Audience or niche
- Optional location

## Process
1. Identify core topic tags.
2. Add niche and audience-intent tags.
3. Avoid misleading or overly broad tags.
4. Group hashtags by strength or purpose.

## Output
- Recommended hashtag sets
- Optional groupings: broad, niche, branded, local

## Quality bar
- Relevance beats volume.
- Avoid repetitive spam patterns.
- Tailor to the platform and content type.
