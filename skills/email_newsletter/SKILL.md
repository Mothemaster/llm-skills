# Email Newsletter

## Purpose
Use this skill to write newsletters for announcements, education, product updates, or community engagement.

## Inputs
- Newsletter goal
- Audience
- Main stories or updates
- CTA
- Brand tone

## Process
1. Prioritize the most important message.
2. Build a clear narrative or structure.
3. Keep sections concise.
4. End with a direct CTA.

## Output
- Subject lines
- Preview text
- Newsletter body copy
- Optional section variations

## Quality bar
- Keep it readable and focused.
- Avoid burying the main message.
- Match the brand voice consistently.
